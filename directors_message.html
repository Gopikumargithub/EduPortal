Dr. Maganti Venkata Ramana Rao
Chairman, Mic College of Technology

Our college has emerged as one of the premier institutions in the region through our relentless efforts in the cause of education. I welcome you to the world of MIC where you will have an opportunity to learn technologies of the future, in the rich academic ambiance typical of MIC. Welcome aboard to enjoy the fruits of holistic education!

Henry David Thoreau said, “Nothing succeeds like enthusiasm.” The Quality of enthusiasm contains a tremendous power to produce vigor, vitality, and joyfulness. It surmounts adversity and difficulty.

Greatness and excellence in any field is achieved through enriched enthusiasm, meticulous planning, measured steps in the right direction and proactive approach. Banking on success in one field, a few successful people cast a wider net into other areas of expertise. MIC is blessed to have the visionary leadership of Dr. MV. Ramana Rao, an educationist-turned-entrepreneur par excellence who has carried his Midas’ touch into both industry and academia. His exposure to the industrial and the educational sectors goaded him to establish an institute in the pristine ambience of Kanchikacherla with the sole aim of achieving a symbiosis of industry and education. Truly, his vision met with great success as evidenced in the fact that thousands of our alumni are well-placed in their career and in life.
